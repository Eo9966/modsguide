FarmHelperEasy

Helper Mod for Easy Resource Gathering

This mod makes it easier to get bone meal than ever before, which is very helpful for gardeners and adds more meaning to grass and flowers. Simplify your farming and resource gathering with minimal effort.

Key Features:

  • Low performance impact
  • One-click setup for quick installation
  • Configurable automation settings
  • Compatible with Minecraft Forge 1.20.1 and NeoForge 1.20.4

How to obtain Dry Grass:

  • Break Tall Grass - 20% drop chance
